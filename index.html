The reality

Your best content lives everywhere: different email accounts, different apps, different inboxes. Opening each one, scanning for the gist, deciding what’s worth saving—that decision fatigue costs more than the reading itself.

Sound familiar?

You want to keep up with the best thinking: Substack writers you trust, deep dives on Medium, SVPG and Maven for product and growth, the OpenAI blog and other company blogs for what’s new. Each lives in a different app, a different inbox, a different tab. By the time you’ve opened everything, you’re out of time—or you give up and the list grows.

What if all of it landed in one place? One feed, one quick skim, one decision at a time. That’s dAIgest.
